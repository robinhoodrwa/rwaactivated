        What this is — and is not

        • Testnet only. Facilities settle in test USDG on Robinhood Chain testnet (chain 46630). No real money moves.
        • Not affiliated. This is an independent protocol experiment. It is not built, endorsed, or operated by Robinhood Markets.
        • Proof of statements, not of title. Passport scans and claim digests attest that a named issuer said something at a point in time. They do not prove title, ownership, or value.
        • No seizure, no liquidation. If a borrower stops repaying, the lender's only protocol action is to release and close the facility. Legal recourse lives offchain.
        • Evidence stays offchain. Only digests are recorded. Inspection reports, insurance certificates, and legal opinions remain with the parties.
        • Permissionless test registry. The registry does not authenticate who chose a physical ID or prevent separate passports from claiming the same one. A collision can block facility activation; production use needs curated issuers and dispute governance.
        • Concurrency, not liens. One object can back only one active facility at a time — a protocol-only rule that binds after escrow is fully funded and the borrower accepts delivery. It is not a lien registry or notice to third parties.

        Proof-to-Capital

        Capital that waits for proof

        ProofGate is an escrow with a checklist. Five parties, one machine, one rule: no proof, no draw.

        1. Lender opens a facility. Names the borrower, the equipment vendor to be paid, the object passport, the principal, and exactly which issuers must attest what.
        2. Lender escrows test USDG. Principal sits in the contract — not with the borrower, not with the vendor.
        3. Issuers publish claims. Counsel, inspector, insurer, custodian — each publishes a digest of their signed statement, with an expiry. Only the named issuer address can.
        4. Borrower accepts delivery. Verified against the live passport registry: right object, unrevoked anchor, borrower in control.
        5. The gate opens. canDraw re-checks everything onchain. If a single claim is missing, expired, revoked, or from the wrong address, the draw reverts.
        6. Draw pays the vendor. Principal moves to the pre-approved payee only. Repayment flows back to the lender and closes the facility.
