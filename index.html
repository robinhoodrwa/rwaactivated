03 / Protocol flow

From a real object
to world-scale digital state.

  1. 01

    Capture

    Reference scans, identifiers, title, rights, custody, inspection, provenance, and other source evidence.

    Source evidence
  2. 02

    Identify

    Create or reuse an object identifier and record the exact recognition method, parameters, source digest, and outputs.

    Physical identity
  3. 03

    Attest

    Require accountable parties to sign narrowly scoped claims with timestamps, authority, status, and expiry.

    Accountable claims
  4. 04

    Anchor

    Fingerprint the versioned passport and publish resolvable state without moving every private document onchain.

    Portable state
  5. 05

    Activate

    Let applications apply transfer, finance, governance, servicing, and compliance policy to verified object state.

    Programmable apps

06 / Design principles

Build for billions of objects.
Earn trust one claim at a time.

01

Object before token

A token cannot make an object, right, or document real. Establish identity and evidence first.

02

State survives apps

Object history should outlive a marketplace, custodian, issuer, wallet, or chain.

03

Claims stay accountable

Every attestation retains its signer, scope, timestamp, source, and current status.

04

Finance is a layer

Markets can use verified state; they do not replace title, custody, law, or physical truth.
