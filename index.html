04 / Open infrastructure

State that survives
the app above it.

RWA Protocol separates identity and accountable evidence from issuance, custody, financing, and markets. Applications can compose verified state without any one application owning the truth.

  • 01
    Evidence Passport

    Versioned manifest with deterministic canonicalization and local structural checks.

  • 02
    Passport Registry

    Current state, history, controller transfer, revocation, and chain-neutral resolution.

  • 03
    Physical Identity Adapter

    Method, parameters, source digest, recognition outputs, confidence, and version.

  • 04
    Robinhood Chain Anchor

    Permissionless EVM contracts with a public testnet path and no mandatory custodian.
